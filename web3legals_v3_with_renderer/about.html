Who I Am

The intersection of law and blockchain is where I live

I'm Rahul Pareek — a Web3 and crypto lawyer based in Jodhpur, India, working with founders and startups globally. I founded Web3Legals to be the legal partner that Web3 builders actually need: one who understands the technology, not just the paperwork around it.

I hold a double gold medal in LLM from the National Law University, with specializations in International Law and Business Law. But more importantly, I've spent years studying blockchain technology, DeFi protocols, token economics, and the rapidly evolving regulatory frameworks surrounding them.

My clients range from solo founders pre-token launch to DAOs managing multi-million dollar treasuries — all united by the same need: legal clarity in an unclear world.

Philosophy

Why I started Web3Legals

I watched too many brilliant founders build incredible technology — only to have it shut down, frozen, or destroyed by legal problems that were entirely preventable.

The Web3 space has unique legal needs that generic corporate lawyers don't understand. When a lawyer doesn't know what a DAO is, they can't protect the people running one.

Web3Legals exists to close that gap — to be the legal partner that actually understands the decentralized world and can protect the people building it.

My commitment to clients

Clear advice in plain language. No unnecessary legal jargon. No billing for every five-minute call. A genuine partner in your project's success — not just a vendor who files documents.
