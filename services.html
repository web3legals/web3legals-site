Our Services

Legal services built
for Web3 natively

Every service is designed from the ground up for the decentralized economy — not adapted from traditional corporate law templates.

Core Service

Token Legal Advisory & Classification

The most critical early-stage legal decision for any Web3 project. We evaluate your token under the Howey Test, Reves Test, and applicable MiCA frameworks.

  • Formal legal opinion on token classification
  • Howey Test & Reves Test analysis
  • Whitepaper legal audit & compliance review
  • SAFT/SAFE agreement drafting
  • Jurisdictional entity selection (UAE, Singapore, BVI, India)
  • SEC, MiCA & SEBI compliance roadmap
🏛
High Priority

DAO Legal Wrappers & Governance

Protect your DAO contributors from personal liability. We structure legal wrappers and draft governance constitutions aligned with your on-chain rules.

  • Wyoming DAO LLC formation
  • Cayman Foundation setup
  • Marshall Islands DAO registration
  • DAO Constitution & governance documentation
  • Member liability framework
  • Treasury management legal structure

Essential

Web3 Terms, Privacy & Disclaimers

Generic SaaS templates are incompatible with Web3 risk profiles. We draft bespoke legal documents that actually protect your platform.

Compliance

KYC/AML & Sanctions Compliance

  • FATF Travel Rule implementation
  • OFAC & global sanctions screening setup
  • Transaction monitoring policy
  • AML risk assessment
  • India FIU-IND PMLA compliance

Founder & Contributor Agreements

  • Contributor & advisor agreements
  • Non-disclosure agreements
  • Non-compete & non-solicitation clauses
