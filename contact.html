FAQ

Common questions

Is the initial consultation really free?

Yes. The first 30-minute regulatory risk assessment is complimentary. There is no obligation to engage further services.

Do you work with projects outside India?

Yes. We advise clients across India, UAE, Singapore, the Cayman Islands, BVI, the USA, and EU. Our practice is globally oriented by design.

How quickly can I get a legal opinion?

For urgent matters, we can often turn around a preliminary opinion within 48–72 hours. Standard timelines depend on scope and complexity.

Do you work on retainer?

Yes. For growing Web3 teams, a monthly retainer arrangement provides ongoing access, priority response, and continuous compliance monitoring.

What information should I prepare before the call?

A brief overview of your project, your token model (if applicable), your target markets and user base, and any specific regulatory concerns or deadlines you're facing.
